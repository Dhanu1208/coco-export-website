About Our Company

Why Choose Our Premium Cocopeat?

Cocopeat, also known as coir pith, is a 100% natural byproduct of coconut husk processing. Our cocopeat is washed, buffered, and compressed to meet strict quality standards. With excellent water retention, low electrical conductivity (EC), and a balanced pH of 5.5-6.8, it is the preferred growing medium for greenhouse cultivation, hydroponics, and nursery applications across India.

Low EC & Balanced pH

EC below 1 mS/cm and pH 5.5–6.8 — ideal for sensitive crops and hydroponic systems

High Water Retention

Holds up to 8–9 times its weight in water, reducing irrigation frequency significantly

Weed & Pathogen Free

Naturally sterile — free from weeds, soil-borne pathogens, and harmful microorganisms

100% Organic & Sustainable

Made from coconut husk — a renewable agricultural byproduct, fully biodegradable
